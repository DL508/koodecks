Frequently Asked Questions

What is KooDeck?

KooDeck turns any YouTube video into a colorful one-page study "deck" — the big ideas, key points, and a quick quiz — so students can review in minutes.

Do students need an account?

No. Anyone can make a few decks a day for free with no account. Teachers and parents can create a free account to save and find their decks, and upgrade for unlimited decks.

How many decks can I make for free?

Free use (with or without an account) includes 3 decks per day. The Unlimited plan removes the daily limit — see Pricing.

What is Daily Dash?

A quick 5-question quiz game. You can also turn any deck you make into a playable Dash and share it — see All Dashes and Daily Dash.

Why did my video say "no captions"?

KooDeck reads a video's captions/subtitles. If a video has none, paste the text yourself using the "paste the text instead" option, or pick a video that has captions.

Are the decks always accurate?

Decks are AI-generated summaries. They're a great starting point, but always double-check important facts with your teacher or the original source.

Is it safe for kids?

Yes. The content filter skips inappropriate material, students don't need to share any personal information to create a deck, and there are no ads.
