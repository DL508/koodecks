How it works

🔗

1 · Paste a link

Any YouTube video with captions — a lesson, an explainer, a documentary clip.

🎯

2 · Pick a grade

K–2, 3–5, 6–8, or 9–12. The reading level and words match the age.

🎨

3 · Share the deck

Name it, choose a fun theme, and send one link — post it to Google Classroom in a tap, or save it as an image.
